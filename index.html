Benefits of Nuts


"Nuts are widely touted for their great taste and convenience, but their taste isn’t the only thing that makes them popular. There are many varieties of nuts.Nuts are a nutritionally rich food, containing most of the vitamins and minerals the body needs. Nuts are largely composed of fats, which might seem counterintuitive for weight loss. However, the fats in nuts are almost entirely unsaturated, which means they leave you feeling more satisfied after eating, helping you eat less over time."
